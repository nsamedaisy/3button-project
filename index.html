Fish Pie

Crust And Tastiest Filling

Ingredient For Dough And Filling

  1. 4 cups all-purpose flour 500g and 1 cup butter 226g
  2. 1 ½ teaspoons salt and ½ teaspoon baking powder
  3. 1 egg - beaten and 1 cup cold water
  4. 1 pound ground beef about half kg,1 medium onion chopped, 1 stem green onions - chopped sub with leeks
  5. 4 large garlic cloves peeled and minced, 1 large bay leaf, ½ teaspoon ground white pepper, 1 habanero pepper optional and 2 medium Irish potatoes -1 cup chopped
